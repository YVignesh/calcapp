Calc Studio - Free Online Calculators for Math, Finance, Units and Health

Calc Studio brings 54 calculators and converters into one fast app for students, everyday users and professionals. Use it for finance, scientific math, graphing, unit conversion, health estimates, cooking conversions, home projects and common percentage or date calculations.

Popular Calculators

  • Compound Interest Calculator for investment growth, monthly deposits, APY and interest earned.
  • Loan Calculator for monthly payments, total interest and amortization schedules.
  • Mortgage Calculator for principal, interest, tax and insurance estimates.
  • Credit Card Payoff Calculator for debt payoff time and interest cost.
  • BMI Calculator and BMR Calculator for health and calorie estimates.
  • Percentage Calculator, Scientific Calculator and Graphing Calculator for school and technical work.
  • Length Converter and other unit converters for practical measurement conversions.

Why Use Calc Studio?

The app focuses on clear answers with useful context: calculation steps, assumptions, charts and breakdowns where they help. It is designed for mobile, tablet and desktop browsers, and most tools run directly on your device.
